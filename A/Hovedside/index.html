Block Clicker

In the game "Blockclicker" it's all about clicking as much as possible and getting the highest score!
As your score increases, you can buy cool new backgrounds and other effects.
Go and try it out!

Tetris

In playing Tetris, you have to make long rows of different types of building blocks. The building blocks consist of four small blocks. As the player levels up, it becomes more and more difficult.
Try it out then!

Block Breaker

In "Block Breaker" it's all about breaking all the blocks.
Each level there will be more blocks, and the ball will move faster! Use the left and right arrow-key to move the player.
Good luck!
