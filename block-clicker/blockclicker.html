Block Clicker

Welcome to block clicker! You earn score by left-clicking on the big block. Once you have clicked a lot, you will start making great discoveries. You can also change backgrounds in the backgrounds menu.

The main source of inspiration for this game, is the many clicker games out there, such as cookie clicker, og muscle clicker. Made by Peder Eggemoen, Sondre Røed, Marius Gunnerød and Olav Aase
